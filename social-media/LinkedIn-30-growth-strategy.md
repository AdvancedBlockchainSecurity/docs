## 2. Viral Content Formula for Web3 Security Companies

**The Carousel Dominance Strategy:**

Research analyzing 2M+ LinkedIn posts reveals carousels generate 6.60% engagement rates—278% more than video and 303% more than images. **Post 3-4 carousels weekly** using this structure: Slide 1 must stop the scroll with shocking stats ("$773K stolen yesterday—here's the vulnerability everyone missed"), slides 2-7 deliver technical breakdown with diagrams, slide 8 provides actionable prevention steps, slide 9 includes clear CTA. Keep text to 25-50 words per slide, use vertical format (performs 20% better than square).

**Hook Formulas That Drive Viral Distribution:**

The first 2-3 lines determine whether users click "see more." These five hook types perform best for Web3 security:

**Shocking Dollar Amount Hook:** "$1.5 billion stolen from ByBit. North Korean hackers exploited one vulnerability. Here's what went wrong:"

**Contrarian Statement Hook:** "Most DeFi protocols are auditing the wrong things. After analyzing 50+ exploits, we found 80.5% came from off-chain attacks—not smart contract bugs."

**Mystery/Curiosity Hook:** "One line of Solidity code saved our client $12M last week. Most developers overlook it:"

**Timely Incident Hook:** "The Hyperdrive Protocol lost $773K yesterday. The router contract vulnerability was preventable. Thread on what auditors should catch:"

**Personal Vulnerability Hook:** "I almost missed a critical reentrancy vulnerability that would have cost our client $5M. Here's what I learned about security blind spots:"

**Content Formats Ranked by Performance:**

- Carousels/Multi-image: 6.60% engagement rate (BEST)
- Native Videos: 5.60% (shoot 60-second explainers with captions)
- Polls: 4.40% engagement (use weekly)
- Text-only: 4.00% (use with exceptional hooks only)

**Post Length and Formatting:**

LinkedIn's algorithm prioritizes dwell time—how long users view your content. Posts between 1,800-2,100 characters see peak reach. Use line breaks every 2-3 sentences, bullet points with arrow symbols (→ ↳), and white space liberally (boosts performance 25%). End every post with a thought-provoking question to drive comments.

**Hashtag Strategy for 2025:**

Use **3-5 hashtags maximum**. Combine broad reach (#Blockchain, #Cybersecurity, #Web3) with niche targeting (#SmartContractSecurity, #DeFiSecurity, #Web3Audits). Use Pascal Case for readability: #BlockchainSecurity not #blockchainsecurity. Place at post end.

**The Optimal Posting Frequency:**

For rapid growth from 16 followers, post **5 times weekly minimum**—Monday through Friday, once daily. Never post twice in the same day (30% reach penalty). The algorithm rewards consistency and topic authority.

**Best Times for Global Web3 Audience:**

Post at **8-9 AM EST Tuesday-Thursday**—captures US morning and late-day Europe simultaneously. Avoid weekends. Schedule carousels for Tuesday 8 AM (peak engagement day), videos for Wednesday 9 AM, polls for Thursday 8 AM.
