# BlockSecOps Pricing Tiers

**Last Updated**: January 3, 2026

## Overview

BlockSecOps offers a premium smart contract security platform with 17+ integrated scanners, ML-powered intelligence, and complete vulnerability management. Our land-and-expand pricing makes entry accessible for developers while delivering enterprise-grade value for growing teams.

**Value Proposition**: Same price as competitors with 1 scanner, but you get 17+ scanners, intelligence layer, and a complete dashboard.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I switch tiers mid-cycle?**
A: Yes, upgrades are prorated. Downgrades take effect at renewal.

**Q: What counts as a "scan"?**
A: One scan = running all selected scanners on one contract/project. Re-scans count as new scans.

**Q: Can I add users without upgrading?**
A: Yes, additional users are $100/user/month on Startup+ tiers.

**Q: Do you offer startup discounts?**
A: Yes, 50% off first year for YC, a16z, and other accelerator companies.

**Q: Is there a non-profit discount?**
A: Yes, contact sales for 50% discount on any tier.
