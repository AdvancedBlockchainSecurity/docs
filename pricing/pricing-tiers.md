## Developer Tier (Free)

**Price**: $0/month

**Target Customer**:
- Developers evaluating the platform
- Open-source project maintainers
- Students and learners
- Solo developers starting out

**Included Features**:
- 3 contracts per month
- 2 user seats
- Public repositories only
- All 25+ scanner integrations enabled
- Basic vulnerability detection (top 20 SWC categories)
- GitHub/GitLab CI integration
- Standard scan speed (results in ~30 minutes)
- Community Discord support

**Limitations**:
- No private repository scanning
- No ML-powered false positive filtering
- No compliance reports
- No priority support
- Community support only

**Purpose**: Generous free tier builds community, generates word-of-mouth, and creates switching costs. Limiting contracts/month (not features) follows Snyk's successful model.

**Conversion Trigger**: 14-day reverse trial exposes users to premium features on signup, then drops to free tier limits. Research shows reverse trials achieve highest conversion rates.

## Free Tier Strategy: 14-Day Reverse Trial

**How It Works**:
1. New users get full Team-tier features for 14 days
2. After trial, account drops to Free tier limits
3. Users experience premium features they'll miss when downgraded
4. Creates strong conversion motivation

**Target Metrics**:
- Free-to-paid conversion: 4-5% (developer tool benchmark)
- Trial-to-paid conversion: 10-15% (reverse trial benchmark)
- Time to first scan: <10 minutes (critical for activation)

## FAQ

**Q: Can I switch tiers mid-cycle?**
A: Yes, upgrades are prorated. Downgrades take effect at renewal.

**Q: What counts as a "contract"?**
A: One contract = one Solidity/Vyper/Rust file scanned. Re-scans count as new scans against your limit.

**Q: Can I add users without upgrading?**
A: Yes, additional users are $29/user/month on any paid tier.

**Q: Do you offer startup discounts?**
A: Yes, 30% off for startups with <$2M raised. Contact sales.

**Q: Is there a non-profit discount?**
A: Yes, 50% discount on any tier for non-profits and educational institutions.

**Q: What happens if I exceed my contract limit?**
A: You can purchase additional contracts at $19/contract or upgrade to the next tier.

**Q: How does the 14-day trial work?**
A: All new accounts get full Team-tier features for 14 days, then drop to Free tier. No credit card required to start.
